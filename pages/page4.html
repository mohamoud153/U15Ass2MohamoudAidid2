Accessibility

Pulse VR is committed to being inclusive for all visitors

Site Display Settings

Adjust the site to suit your needs

♿ Physical Accessibility

Our centre is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps, wide corridors, and accessible VR stations. All gaming pods are designed to accommodate visitors with mobility impairments, and our staff are always on hand to assist.

👁 Visual Accessibility

We offer high contrast mode and adjustable text size on this website. Inside the centre, all signage uses high contrast colours with large text. Staff assistance is always available, and our VR games include colourblind-friendly modes.

👂 Hearing Accessibility

All VR games with audio content include subtitles or visual cues. Our staff are trained to communicate effectively with visitors who are deaf or hard of hearing, and a hearing loop is available at reception.

🧠 Neurodiversity

We offer quiet sessions every Tuesday morning for visitors who are sensitive to noise and busy environments. Our staff are trained in supporting neurodivergent visitors, and we can adjust lighting and audio levels on request.

🌐 Screen Reader Compatibility

This website is built with semantic HTML5 and ARIA labels to ensure full compatibility with screen readers such as NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver. All images include descriptive alt text.
